CRITICAL distinctions (Pitfall 4 from RESEARCH.md):
- `:library` uses `compose.desktop.common` — platform-neutral JAR — and `explicitApi()`.
- `:showcase` uses `compose.desktop.currentOs` — pulls native Skiko binary — and does NOT have `explicitApi()`.
- Showcase MUST depend on library via `implementation(project(":library"))` — NOT a Maven coordinate.

Avoid:
- Do NOT add `explicitApi()` to showcase (causes spurious "Visibility must be specified" errors on internal helper composables).
- Do NOT use `compose.desktop.currentOs` in `:library` (forces OS-native binary into the published JAR).
- Do NOT skip `kotlinx-coroutines-core` — it is in the standard stack and Phase 2 animations will need it.
